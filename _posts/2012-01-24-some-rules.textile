h1. {{ page.title }}

p=(big). The best solution to a problem is not to have it.

p=. An insufficiently ugly temporary hack is permanent.

p=. There is no such thing as standby infrastructure: there is stuff you always use and stuff that won't work when you need it.

p=. The first fallacy of automation is making machines perform each step of a manual human process.

p=. These are not features: Security, Availability, Performance.
