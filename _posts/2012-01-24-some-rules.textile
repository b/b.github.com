h1. {{ page.title }}

p=. (big)The best solution to a problem is not to have it.

p=. (big)An insufficiently ugly temporary hack is permanent.

p=. (big)There is no such thing as standby infrastructure: there is stuff you always use and stuff that won't work when you need it.

p=. (big)The first fallacy of automation is making machines perform each step of a manual human process.

p=. (big)These are not features: Security, Availability, Performance.
